<objective>
Fix the "View switching loads correct data" UAT failure (Test 6). Rapid view switches are dropped because: (1) the $effect bridge coalesces intermediate URL values (Svelte 5 batches effects to next microtask), and (2) a stale guard `viewName === viewCtx.activeView` blocks return-to-current-view clicks. The fix: `handleViewChange` calls `queue.enqueue()` directly (bypassing URL/$effect), and the URL is updated as a side-effect after the handler completes.

Purpose: Close UAT Gap 2 — ensure every view switch click produces exactly one queued VIEW_CHANGE event.
Output: Direct enqueue path for view changes, URL-as-side-effect pattern in EventHandler.
</objective>
